💶 Money: Ireland uses Euro (€). Credit cards accepted everywhere. Tap to pay works at most places. Carry €50–100 cash for pubs and small spots.
🚗 Driving: Drive on the LEFT. Country roads are narrow — take it slow. Google Maps works great. Roundabouts yield to traffic already in the circle.
📱 Phones: US phones work on international plans. T-Mobile has free Ireland data. AT&T/Verizon charge ~$10/day. Download offline Google Maps for County Clare before you go.
🌦️ Weather: July is mild — 60–68°F. Pack a light rain jacket. You WILL need it at least once. Sunshine and showers often happen the same hour.
🍺 Pubs: McDermott's, O'Looney's, and The Nineteenth are the classic Lahinch pubs. Ask for a pint of Guinness and give it the full 2-minute pour. Traditional music sessions happen spontaneously — usually Fri/Sat nights.
⏰ Time: Ireland is 5 hours ahead of Eastern. Sunrise is around 5:15 AM in July — it stays light until 10 PM. Don't fight jet lag — just go with it.
🏔️ Cliffs of Moher: Go early (before 10 AM) or late (after 4 PM) to beat tour buses. €8/person entry. The cliff walk toward O'Brien's Tower is the best view.
